Durable & Cost-Effective Packaging Films

Our 3-layer co-extruded LDPE, MDPE, and LLDPE films are a popular corrugated box replacement for bundling bottles, cans, and tetra packs. With high clarity from 50 microns onwards, they provide significant cost savings over traditional packaging methods.

Convenient & Spacious Handle Bags

Our external flexible handle bags are designed for more storage and easy carrying. Perfect for retail, trade shows, seminars, and promotions, these bags feature internally attached flexible handles for enhanced comfort and convenience.

Eco-Friendly PE/PE Bags & Rolls

Our PE/PE bags & rolls are 100% recyclable, thanks to their mono-material structure. Designed for sustainability, they serve as an excellent alternative to conventional laminates that are difficult to recycle.

Durable & Secure Bubble Mailers

Made from high-strength polymers, our bubble mailers are tear and weather-resistant. With a permanent adhesive seal, they are ideal for online shopping deliveries and various industries. Widely used by courier, parcel, and e-commerce companies, they ensure cushioned protection during transit.

Lightweight & Secure Poly Mailers

Our poly mailers are designed for the safe and reliable transport of documents and consignments, making them the preferred choice for courier, parcel, and e-commerce companies. Durable & tear-resistant for added security. Ideal for online retailers, logistics providers, and businesses, these mailers ensure cost-effective and efficient packaging.

Durable & Versatile Side Seal Bags

Our side seal bags feature heat-sealed edges with a folded bottom, ensuring strength and durability. They can be customized with handles, air holes, and bottom gussets before the sealing process, offering various design variations. Perfect for retail, food, and industrial packaging, these bags provide a reliable and efficient solution.
